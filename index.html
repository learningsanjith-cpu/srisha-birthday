Happy Birthday, Darling

happy birthday darlinggggg

19????? that actually sounds fake because in my head you're still this tiny chaotic little thing that says the most out of pocket stuff ever and then acts innocent after 😭

but genuinelyyy happy birthdayyy my darlingggg

i love youuu so so so muchhh and i really hope this year is soft and kind to you and gives you everything you deserve — because you deserve so much more than you think.

and idk how, but somehow you became one of the most important parts of my life so quietly.

like at first it was just us sitting behind oat talking nonsense and suddenly now you're the person i think about when literally anything happens.

like i'll hear a romantic song and immediately think of you even if i don't understand the language 😭

or i'll see something funny and instinctively think “nahhh srisha would laugh at this” and then i laugh by myself like an idiot.

and i swear my heart does this little “!” every time i see you.

especially after not seeing you for a while — because then you do that thing where you squish my cheeks or pinch me and idk how to explain it but it genuinely feels comforting. like i'm home again.

and omg the way you grab my neck and pull me closer to kiss your cheek. actually unfair. because my brain stops working every single time.

and you have no idea how cute you are when you do little things. like picking up that ladybug so gently omg 😭 i remember seeing that and just thinking — wow, she's actually so soft inside.

and your baby pics. OH MY GOD. you were the cutest darling ever — i actually wanted to eat you 😭😭😭 especially that flower eating pic — i genuinely died when i saw it.

and okay, Pune. i never told you this properly but when i had that gut feeling something was wrong — i didn't even think. i just wanted to come to you. and i'm so glad i did. because getting to take care of you meant a lot to me. even if i was sitting there crying like some romcom character 😭 don't expose me pls.

and Holi omg 😭 i was SOOO gone but somehow the only thing keeping me alive was you and your perfume. i was literally holding onto you for dear life. sorry about that but also not really 😌

and those train rides with you. idk why but even small things feel nice with you. like waiting till your metro left or just sitting next to you doing nothing — it all means way more to me than it should.

and i didn't even tell you this but after your metro left i was lowkey so lost 😭

and okay — those nights where we just cuddle and sleep next to each other. actually some of my favourite moments ever. just lying there with you felt so peaceful and warm and safe. even though you STOLE the bedsheet 😐 i'll forgive you because you're cute.

and i love the way you kiss my cheek when i get hurt — like that's your official solution to everything 😭 or the way you hit me and then act like nothing happened. or when you make me repeat “chi” properly till you're satisfied 😭 or when you squeeze my cheeks like i'm some stress toy. or when you whisper things and then laugh at my reaction. or the look before we kiss at the end of the day — omg it melts me every single time.

and your voice. darling, your voice genuinely warms my heart. like even if i'm tired or annoyed or stressed — hearing you instantly makes things feel softer.

and i know i joke a lot and ramble and annoy you constantly. but genuinely — i love loving you. i love caring about you. i love hearing about your day and your random thoughts and your stories and your opinions on literally anything. i even love when you bully me a little because somehow it's cute when you do it 😭

and honestly i still think about that night on the bench when i asked “srisha may i be your boyfriend” and you said yes. like what 😭 still crazy to me.

and now i get to wish you happy birthday as YOUR boyfriend. which feels really special to me.

and idk what this next year is going to look like. but i do know that no matter what — i've got you. always. through your chaos, through your moods, through your overthinking, through your soft moments. through everything.

because you matter to me so much.

and i really hope 19 treats you gently.

i hope you laugh a lot this year.
i hope you feel loved every single day.
i hope you get everything your heart wants.

and selfishly — i hope i get to be beside you through all of it.

okay i'm done now before i become TOO soft 😭

happy birthday my darling.
thank you for existing.
i love you so so so so much 🦋
